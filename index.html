Great Wall of China (China)

An ancient series of walls and fortifications, the Great Wall is one of the most iconic structures in the world, stretching over 13,000 miles.

Petra (Jordan)

An archaeological site famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system, Petra is often called the "Rose City" due to the color of the stone from which it is carved.

Christ the Redeemer (Brazil)

A massive statue of Jesus Christ located in Rio de Janeiro, standing at 98 feet tall on the Corcovado mountain, overlooking the city.

Machu Picchu (Peru)

An ancient Incan city set high in the Andes Mountains, Machu Picchu is renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone construction and breathtaking panoramic views.

Roman Colosseum (Italy)

An ancient amphitheater located in the center of Rome, the Colosseum is one of the greatest works of Roman engineering and architecture, known for hosting gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

Taj Mahal (India)

A white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, the Taj Mahal was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal and is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world.
